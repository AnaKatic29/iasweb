Emeritus Professor, IAS Member

Badar Alam Iqbal

Emeritus Professor Badar Alam Iqbal; (Ph.D. & DBA; M.Com; DSW; B.A. [Hons] in Economics) had been a DAAD Fellow at the German Institute of Economic Research in Berlin, Kiel Institute of World Economics in Kiel, and the South Asia Institute at the University of Heidelberg in Germany. He has been associated with the Ford Foundation and the American Research Center in the USA, as well as serving as a Fulbright Visiting Professor in the USA and a scholar at the Institute of Developing Economies in Tokyo. Currently, Dr. Iqbal is an Emeritus Professor and External-Relation-Chair of Research at the Frederic Bastiat Institute for African Research in Ghana. He is also an Adjunct Professor at the Faculty of Economics and Finance at Monarch University, Switzerland; a Visiting Professor at the University of Rennes1, France; and the University of South Africa. He has held positions such as Extra Ordinary Professor at the School of Economic Sciences at North-West University, South Africa, and Visiting Professor of Research at Vaal University of Technology and International Islamic University Malaysia. In 2021 and 2022, he was recognized by the Vice Chancellors of three leading South African Universities. He has published in various publishers including Routledge, Sage, Emerald, Elsevier, Kluwer, and Springer. Prof. Iqbal began his academic career in 1978 as a Lecturer and progressed to become a Reader, Professor, and Dean. He has also served as Chairman of the Department of Commerce and as Vice Chancellor. He held roles such as Officiating Finance Officer at A.M.U. Aligarh and was a Member of the University Court and University’s Executive Council. He also served as Member Coordinator for the National Assessment and Accreditation Council, Government of India. Prof. Iqbal was also a Member of the Advisory Board at the Department of Economics, Tshwane University of Technology, South Africa. In 2022, QS Global Academic Survey invited Prof. Badar Iqbal to participate in the Global Universities Ranking Survey. In 2023, Times Higher Education; London invited Prof. Badar Iqbal to participate in the upcoming Global Interdisciplinary Research Survey.
