Founder and President of IAS

Prof. Dr. Dragana Mitrović

Dr. Dragana Mitrović, full professor at the Faculty of Political Sciences, University of Belgrade, founder and president of the Institute for Asian Studies (www.ias.rs), is a leading expert in the economics, politics, international relations, and security of contemporary China and East Asia in our region. She is the founder and head of the Center for Asian and Far Eastern Studies at FPNS, as well as the master program in Regional Asian Studies. She is also the founding editor of the journal "Asian Review". Professor Mitrović has a complete university career, from assistant intern to full professor. She has served one mandate in the diplomatic service of the former Federal Republic of Yugoslavia at the Embassy in Beijing as a political affairs advisor (2001, 2002) and at the Embassy of Serbia and Montenegro as a counselor for economic and political affairs (2003, 2004). Professor Mitrović teaches Political Economy, Political Economy of International Relations, Political Economy of the People's Republic of China and East Asia, Geopolitics, and Geo-economics. She is the author of five books, over sixty scientific papers, and several dozens of publicist papers and interviews. Professor Mitrović has participated in more than forty international scientific conferences as an invited speaker. She is a frequent guest in domestic and foreign media as an expert on processes and phenomena in her area of expertise. Since 2008, she has been a member of the Economic Committee of the Serbian Academy of Sciences and Arts.
